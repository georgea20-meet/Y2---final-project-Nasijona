About

our story.

  • Mission & Vision

    Nasijona has three main missions:
    1. empower and support Palestinian women economically, culturally and socially.
    2. resurrect, preserve, and develop local traditions
    3. rstore social harmony to the local Nazareth community

  • Where We Come From

    Nasijona in Nazareth was established at the end of 2014 as a registered nonprofit organization with a twelve member Executive Board. Nasijona was established according to the needs of our local community of Nazareth in a grassroot fashion. We want to use art take care of everything that is precious to us, our community, our culture, our values.

  • Why Are We Different

    Nasijonaâ€™s has the unique value of bringing back the harmony of the social structure by sharing art and culture. There is an urgent need to preserve the diverse identities that can potentially become extinguished; therefore, it is our duty to revive the traditional handcrafts of our people and to improve the social-economic structures of our society.

    We also uniquely open educational opportunities for women in the local community by training them in handcrafts. In this way, we create opportunity for the women in Nazareth to become productive members of society.

  • Socioeconomic Challenges

    Locally, Nasijona address the socioeconomic challenge to empower and support local Palestinian women through employment opportunities. More broadly, Nasijona empowers the entire community of Nazareth by exposing it to the wider region and allow it to enter multiple new markets.

  • What Makes The Company Remarkable

    Nasijona is an organization that was born to address the special needs of the community. It is a grassroots movement to realize the needs, optimize the structure, and restore the harmony of our society.

  • Where We Are Going

    For the next years, Nasijona plas to expand the company, and get more people involved in the production of handcrafts. We want to expand our community outreach to include all kinds of people, including people with special needs, nationalities, and ages. In addition, Nasijona aims to be an even more self-sufficient and self-sustainable organization.

SUMMER CAMP



Date:
First Camp : 7th July - 17th July
Second Camp: 29th July - 7th August

Cost:
300 shekel per person
500 shekel per 2 brothers


Annually, Nasijona is excited to host two summer camps. These summer camps allow the children to learn traditional handmade crafting skills, including embroidery and needlework.
This is an amazing opportunity for the Nazareth community to bond together over cultural heritage through expressions of unique artwork. This gives a unique for the young generation to learn about their traditions from the skilled women in the cultural center.

The camp is hosted by trained professionals who are skilled in Palestinian traditional artwork. For more information, please contact us
